Never Stop Improving

Info

“Never Stop Improving” is an artistic project that continuously displays new Reddit post titles on topics of self-improvement as a never-ending ticker. The content ranges from constructive reflection to a dangerous culture of self-optimization.

Content Note

All content on this website is retrieved live from Reddit and therefore may not be subject to any separate content review beyond Reddit's own content moderation. If you come across content that you find offensive or inappropriate, please contact me at hey@robertziesenis.xyz
